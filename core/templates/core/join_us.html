Tour company listing guidelines

To list your safari company with Your African Safari, please ensure you’ve read and meet our criteria.

Basic requirements

  • You are fully licensed and registered to operate a safari tourism business in the country or countries where the business is incorporated.
  • You are affiliated with the company and possess a company email. ISP email addresses, including those from AOL, Hotmail, Google, Yahoo, BT, GMX and so on will require additional identification and may prevent a company from being listing when clear identification cannot be determined.
  • You have registered as a member of YAS using your professional email address.
  • You specialize in African safaris and primarily offer tours in one or more of these countries.
  • You have a functional website that clearly states the nature of your business.
